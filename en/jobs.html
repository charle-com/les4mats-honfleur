Who are we looking for?

Three profiles in priority. Not exactly one of them but you're keen? Call anyway.

Profile 01

Cook

You master the basics, you love fresh fish and you adapt to a short menu that changes. Fine-dining experience not required, willingness to learn, yes.

Profile 02

Waiter

You know how to welcome guests, you learn the menu by heart within a week and you love sending people away with a big smile. Service is your thing.

Profile 03

All-rounder

You like putting your hands everywhere? Kitchen, floor, dishwashing, supplies. If you're motivated and reliable, we have a spot for you in the crew.
